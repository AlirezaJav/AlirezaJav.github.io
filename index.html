About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Toronto Metropolitan University. I joined TMU in February 2023. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral research fellow at Queen's University. I received my PhD from Instituto Superior Técnico, University of Lisbon in 2021 and my MSc from Sharif University of Technolgy in 2013. My interest are Multimedia Compression, Computer Vision and Deep Learning.
